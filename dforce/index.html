About dForce

dForce advocates to develop and promote blockchain-native DeFi and monetary protocols serving as back-bone infrastructure for DeFi and open finance applications.
dForce is featuring a dual-token model, with USDx as transaction stablecoin and the network token (DF) for providing utility of our open finance platform.
We will build a variety of decentralized protocols including Decentralized Stablecoin (USDx), Dex, Money Market, Lending Market, Financial Derivatives Market, Prediction Market, and etc.

About USDx

USDx is our first monetary protocol, which is a decentralized and synthetic indexed stablecoin 1:1 pegged to a basket of constituent stablecoins, integrated with interest bearing capability.

USDx's initial underlying portfolios include USDC, TUSD, PAX and DAI.

USDx protocol allows its users to use other stablecoins in a pre-determined weighting to mint a synthetic indexed stablecoin and vice versa, to disaggregate into constituent stablecoins fully on-chain to ensure max transparency, immutability and auditability.
